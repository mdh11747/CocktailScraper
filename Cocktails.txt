Spirit: Vodka Cocktails
Title: Dreamy Dorini Smoking Martini
Ingredients: 2 ounces vodka (such as Grey Goose)
Ingredients: 1/2 ounce Laphroaig 10-year-old single malt scotch
Ingredients: 1 dash Pernod
Ingredients: Garnish: lemon twist
Instructions1: Add all ingredients into a mixing glass with ice and stir until well-chilled.
Instructions2: Strain into a cocktail glass.
Instructions3: Express the oils from the lemon twist over the drink, then garnish with the twist.
Title: Farmer’s Cocktail
Ingredients: 3 1-inch-thick cubes watermelon
Ingredients: 3/4 ounce simple syrup
Ingredients: 2 ounces Veev spirit
Ingredients: 1/4 ounce Aperol
Ingredients: 1 ounce tomato juice, freshly pressed
Ingredients: 3/4 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
Ingredients: Garnish: 3 cherry tomatoes, speared
Instructions1: Add the watermelon cubes and simple syrup into a shaker and muddle.
Instructions2: Add the Veev, Aperol, and tomato and lime juices, plus ice, and shake until well-chilled.
Instructions3: Double-strain into a highball glass.
Instructions4: Garnish with three speared cherry tomatoes.
Title: Hairy Navel

Ingredients: 4 ounces orange juice, freshly squeezed
Ingredients: Garnish: orange wheel
Ingredients: Garnish: preserved cherry
Instructions1: Add the vodka, peach schnapps and orange juice into a Collins glass with ice and stir briefly to combine.
Instructions2: Garnish with an orange wheel and a preserved cherry.
Title: Chi Chi

Ingredients: 4 ounces pineapple juice

Instructions3: Garnish with a pineapple slice.
Title: Bay Breeze

Ingredients: Garnish: lime wheel

Title: Chocolate Martini
Ingredients: Cocoa powder, to rim glass
Ingredients: 1 ounce chocolate liqueur (such as Godiva), plus more for rimming glass
Ingredients: 1 ounce creme de cacao (such as Giffard)
Ingredients: 1 ounce vodka
Ingredients: 1/2 ounce half-and-half
Instructions1: Add a small amount of chocolate liqueur into a small rimmed plate. Add a small amount of cocoa powder to another plate. Dip the rim of an inverted cocktail glass into the liqueur, then into the cocoa powder. Set aside.
Instructions2: Add the chocolate liqueur, creme de cacao, vodka and half-and-half into a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
Instructions3: Strain into the prepared glass.
Title: Pom-Blood-Orange Old Fashioned
Ingredients: 2 lengths of blood orange peel
Ingredients: 1 teaspoon pomegranate arils (seeds)
Ingredients: 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
Ingredients: 2 dashes Angostura bitters
Ingredients: 2 ounces vodka (such as Grey Goose)
Ingredients: Garnish: blood orange twist
Instructions1: Add the blood orange peels, pomegranate arils, sugar and bitters into a shaker and muddle gently.
Instructions2: Add the vodka and fill with ice, and shake until well-chilled.
Instructions3: Double-strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.
Instructions4: Garnish with an orange twist.

Title: Holiday at the Hive
Ingredients: 3/4 ounce hot water
Ingredients: 1/2 ounce honey
Ingredients: 1 ounce Grey Goose L’Orange vodka
Ingredients: 1/2 ounce triple sec or Grand Marnier
Ingredients: 1/2 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed
Ingredients: 1/2 ounce orange juice, freshly squeezed
Ingredients: 1 pinch star anise powder
Instructions1: Add the hot water and honey into a shaker and stir until the honey is dissolved.
Instructions2: Add the vodka, triple sec or Grand Marnier, lemon juice, orange juice and star anise powder with ice, and shake vigorously until well-chilled.
Instructions3: Strain into a cocktail glass.
Title: Anarchist’s Pop Shoppe
Ingredients: 1 ounce vodka
Ingredients: 1/2 ounce Cherry Heering
Ingredients: 1/4 ounce Benedictine
Ingredients: 1 teaspoon lemon zest
Ingredients: 5 ounces soda water
Instructions1: Add the vodka, Cherry Heering, Benedictine and lemon zest into a mixing glass and lightly muddle the lemon zest.
Instructions2: Add ice and stir until well-chilled.
Instructions3: Fine-strain into a Collins glass with ice.
Instructions4: Top with the soda water.
Title: Super Soup

Title: Appletini
Ingredients: 1 1/4 ounces vodka
Ingredients: 1 ounce Calvados
Ingredients: 1 1/4 ounces Granny Smith apple juice
Ingredients: 1/4 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed
Ingredients: 1/4 ounce simple syrup
Ingredients: Garnish: brandied cherry
Ingredients: Garnish: Granny Smith apple slices
Instructions1: Add crushed ice into a cocktail glass and set aside to chill.
Instructions2: Add the vodka, Calvados, apple juice, lemon juice and simple syrup into a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
Instructions3: Discard the ice from the cocktail glass and double-strain the mixture into the glass.
Instructions4: Garnish with a skewered brandied cherry and 3 Granny Smith apple slices.

Title: Tom Yum on a Rock
Ingredients: 1 lime wedge
Ingredients: Red pepper flakes, to rim glass
Ingredients: Sea salt, to rim glass
Ingredients: 1 Makrut lime leaf
Ingredients: 1 stalk lemongrass, chopped, hard outer shell removed
Ingredients: 1 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
Ingredients: 1 3/4 ounces Beluga vodka
Ingredients: 2/3 ounce Cointreau
Ingredients: 2/3 ounce palm sugar syrup (equal parts palm sugar and water, boiled and cooled)
Ingredients: Garnish: lime wheel
Ingredients: Garnish: red chile pepper
Instructions1: Rub the lime wedge on half of the outer rim of a rocks glass. Add equal parts red pepper flakes and sea salt to a small plate, and dip the glass in the mixture.
Instructions2: Add the makrut lime leaf, lemongrass and lime juice into a shaker and muddle gently.
Instructions3: Add the vodka, Cointreau, palm sugar syrup, and ice, and shake until well-chilled.
Instructions4: Double-strain into the prepared glass over fresh ice.
Instructions5: Garnish with a lime wheel and red chile pepper on a bamboo pick.
