Title2: Farmer’s Cocktail
Ingredients1: 3 1-inch-thick cubes watermelon
Ingredients2: 3/4 ounce simple syrup
Ingredients3: 2 ounces Veev spirit
Ingredients4: 1/4 ounce Aperol
Ingredients5: 1 ounce tomato juice, freshly pressed
Ingredients6: 3/4 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
Ingredients7: Garnish: 3 cherry tomatoes, speared

Title6: Chocolate Martini

Ingredients2: 1 ounce chocolate liqueur (such as Godiva), plus more for rimming glass
Ingredients3: 1 ounce creme de cacao (such as Giffard)
Ingredients4: 1 ounce vodka
Ingredients5: 1/2 ounce half-and-half
